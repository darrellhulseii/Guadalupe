✟ The Faith Crosses the Pacific ✟

Our Lady of Guadalupe in the Philippines -- 1601

Nine million Nahua souls converted in seven years -- the greatest mass conversion in history. Now the Augustinians carry Her image across the Pacific. You will play as Fray Miguel, establishing Her devotion in Makati, Manila.

✟ The Mission is Complete ✟

Our Lady of Guadalupe's devotion is established in the Philippines. The Guadalupe Church in Makati stands to this day -- the first on Asian soil. Every December 12, thousands carry candles to the Pasig River, which glows from shore to shore.

The tilma of Juan Diego remains inexplicably intact after nearly five centuries. No scientist has ever explained it. More pilgrims visit Tepeyac each year than any other Marian shrine on earth.
